Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis
End-to-end data analytics project analyzing 3,900 transactions across 18 features to uncover customer purchasing patterns, segment behavior, and drive business recommendations.
3,900 Transactions
18 Features
3 Segments
$233K Total Revenue
Python Pandas SQL PostgreSQL Power BI Seaborn Matplotlib
Key Insights
  • Male customers generated 2× more revenue ($157,890) than female customers ($75,191) across all transactions
  • Loyal segment: 3,116 customers — the dominant cohort driving repeat purchase value
  • Subscription doesn't drive higher per-order spend — $59.87 vs $59.49 avg (non-subs vs subs)
  • 73% non-subscribers contributed ~$170K vs 27% subscribers at $62.6K revenue
  • Young adults led revenue contribution; all age groups had near-equal avg spend of $59.76
  • Review rating imputed by category median; snake_case normalization applied across 18 columns
  • Age group & purchase frequency features engineered for segmentation modeling
Business Recommendations
  • Launch exclusive subscriber perks to boost the 27% subscriber base
  • Build loyalty reward programs targeting the Loyal segment (3,116 customers)
  • Review discount policy to balance sales lift against margin erosion
  • Prioritize high-revenue age groups & express-shipping users in marketing spend
